    What I Bring to Client Work¶

    Econometric Analysis & Causal Inference¶

    I design and execute full econometric pipelines — from raw data to publication-ready results. My toolkit includes ANCOVA, propensity score matching, IPW, IV estimation, panel fixed effects, and sensitivity analysis (Oster bounds). I've applied these methods to evaluate agricultural interventions at scale, replicate QJE-level research (Bilal & Kanzig), and build original datasets for spatial economics.

    Dataset Construction & Spatial Harmonization¶

    I specialize in building large-scale panel datasets from messy administrative sources. This involves harmonizing district/county boundaries across census vintages, imputing missing environmental data via spatial interpolation, and merging across agencies (Census, IRS, EPA, RBI). Datasets I've built cover hundreds of geographic units over decades.

    Quantitative Trading & Financial Engineering¶

    I build production-grade algorithmic trading systems — not backtests that look good on paper, but live bots managing real capital 24/7. My systems include multi-strategy signal detection, portfolio-level risk management with circuit breakers, and comprehensive backtesting with 800+ variant grid searches. Currently running 20 strategies on Hyperliquid perpetual futures.

    Full-Stack & Web3 Development¶

    I build modern web applications using Next.js, TypeScript, and the T3 stack (tRPC, Prisma, Tailwind). My Web3 experience includes DEX frontends with wallet integration (wagmi, ethers.js) and DeFi analytics tools.
